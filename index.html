Introduction

The Mumbra College of Arts & Commerce was founded in 2001 by visionaries and founder of F.M.S. Educational Trust, Mumbra. It is renowed for its academic excellence, exemplary discipline and professional orientation. this college is an institution where knowledge to cope with the tasks that are in store for them but also develop other aspects and personality to sustain themselves in their life. The admission is given as per standard and transparent norms.

Goals

The aim of this college is to encourage and promote the education to socially, economically backward and weaker section of the society. The college also aims to assist students in advancement of their education by given books for studies and awarding prizes
